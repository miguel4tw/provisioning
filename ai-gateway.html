How it works

Three steps to an AI-ready organisation.

Connect once. Set permissions once. Everyone gets exactly the access they should — automatically.

01

Connect your tools

Link your AI models — Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini — and your company systems: your ATS, HRIS, Salesforce, Confluence, Jira. Keys and credentials are held securely in escrow. Nothing lives in a spreadsheet or an inbox.

02

Set permissions by role

Define what each team gets access to. Engineering can query your codebase and Jira. Finance sees cost dashboards and their ERP. Legal gets read-only access to contracts — nothing more. You control the scope; Harriet enforces it.

03

Everyone gets seamless access

Each employee's AI client is automatically provisioned with the right models and the right system connections. New starters are ready from day one. Leavers are cut off instantly. No manual setup. No configuration tickets.

Features

One gateway.
Total control.

Centralised access control

One place to see every employee's AI access, across every model and every connected system. Add, change, or revoke permissions instantly — no tickets, no waiting on vendor support.

Automatic provisioning

New hire joins? Their AI environment is ready before they open their laptop. Role changes? Permissions update automatically. Offboarding? Access is cut off the moment they leave Active Directory.

Works with any MCP-compatible tool

Built on the Model Context Protocol open standard. If your AI client supports MCP — Claude Desktop, Cursor, ChatGPT, or anything else — Harriet Gateway works with it. No vendor lock-in.

Full audit trail

Every connection, query, and data access is logged against an identity — not an API key. When compliance asks who queried the HR system on Tuesday, you have an answer in seconds, not days.
